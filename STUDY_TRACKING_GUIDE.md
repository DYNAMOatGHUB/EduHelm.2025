## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. Use the Pomodoro Technique
- Study for 25 minutes
- Take a 5-minute break
- End session and start a new one
- After 4 sessions, take a longer 15-30 min break

### 2. Add Notes to Sessions
- Summarize what you learned
- Note difficult concepts to review later
- Track your progress through course material
- Helps with spaced repetition

### 3. Set Realistic Goals
- Start small (e.g., 30 min/day)
- Gradually increase as you build the habit
- Don't set goals too high (leads to burnout)
- Consistency > Intensity

### 4. Review Analytics Weekly
- Check your 30-day chart every Sunday
- Identify your most productive days
- Adjust your schedule based on insights
- Celebrate improvements

### 5. Associate Sessions with Courses
- Track time per subject
- See which courses need more attention
- Balance your study time across subjects
- Use course breakdown chart for insights
